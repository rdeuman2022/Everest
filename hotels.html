Thanka Inn Gokyo

Thanka Inn Gokyo features accommodations in Khumjung. Boasting a 24-hour front desk, this property also has a restaurant and a terrace. The inn has family rooms. A continental breakfast is available each morning at the inn. Lukla is 19 mi from Thanka Inn Gokyo, while Nāmche Bāzār is 9.3 mi from the property. The nearest airport is Tumlingtar Airport, 53 mi from the accommodations.

Ss Hotel Kings & Lodge

SS Hotel Kings & Lodge in Charikot has 5-star accommodations with a restaurant and a bar. With a garden, the property also features a terrace. The rooms include a balcony with a city view. Guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Featuring a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries, certain accommodations at SS Hotel Kings & Lodge also feature a mountain view. All rooms at the accommodation are equipped with a seating area. Staff at the reception can provide advice on what to do in the area.

The Garden palace Hotel

Set in Charikot, The garden palace hotel has a restaurant, bar and garden. Private parking can be arranged at an extra charge. All guest rooms in the hotel are fitted with a flat-screen TV. A à la carte breakfast is available every morning at The garden palace hotel. Dhulikhel is 30 mi from the accommodations. The nearest airport is Tribhuvan Airport, 42 mi from The garden palace hotel.
